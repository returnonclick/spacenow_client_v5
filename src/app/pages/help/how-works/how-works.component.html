I want a specific type of space, how can I find it on Spacenow?

 The easiest way to find a Space is using our search function. Located on the homepage, you can filter spaces by their function and further narrow your search by entering the location you wish to find a space in. Still can’t find what you’re looking for? Don’t stress, we’re adding new listings daily. Check back regularly to see if your dream space has been listed with us.

How long will it take for my listing to be approved?

 All listings will be approved within 24hrs as long as they meet the sites standards.

How long will it be until I get my first booking?

 Once you have submitted your listing and it has been approved, it’s completely up to potential renters to contact you. We can’t put a definitive time frame on it and that’s why we don’t charge you for listing with us! Advertise your space for as long as you would like. We definitely recommend a catchy title and high quality images to better attract potential renters.

My space is not an office space, can I still list it?

 Definitely! Here at Spacenow, we want you to be able to share any unused space you have. Have a spare kitchen, car space, function space, clinic room? List it with us! We have extensive categories available online, someone somewhere is looking for the space you have.

Are there any other associated costs, aside from rent, I will have to pay to use Spacenow?

 Nope! There are no hidden fees with Spacenow. You will only have to pay the amount agreed upon between yourself and the host.

Will I be able to inspect the space before I enter into the license agreement?

 We recommend you communicate this in your correspondence with the host prior to entering the license agreement. Space inspections are up to the discretion of the host and may often be unavailable due to their current circumstances.

Are the prices listed negotiable?

 Prices are set by the host and are firm. Prices may change at the host's discretion.

Can I edit my listing even after it has been approved and online?

 Absolutely! Login with your user details, click on your user name once it appears after signing in top right. Use the drop down menu to locate “My Spaces” then select the workspace you wish to edit (if you have multiple listings). Select edit then run through the same steps as listing, all details will be there just edit the details you wish to change.

There are changes to my circumstances! How do I unlist my space?

 Log into your space, go to "My Spaces", select the space (if multiple) and click the deactivate button to the right.

The licensee is not adhering to the House Rules, am I within my right to terminate the agreement?

 Yes.

Is there a cooling off period?

 Cooling off and cancellation periods are defined in the the Cancellation & Refunds Policy for hourly, daily, weekly & monthly bookings.

I’d like to give feedback about a space I’ve rented or a host I’ve rented from, how can I do this?

 At the end of each booking you will receive an email containing a link to your hosts reviews.

Help! The space I want is not available and the host isn't replying to my enquiries.

 Don’t worry, many of our hosts are quite busy and may not have the time to reply to your enquiry immediately. Unfortunately, due to demand of spaces sometimes you may not be able to obtain the space you would like. We list a variety of spaces and recommend you look for several options with us.

I’ve submitted an enquiry, am I guaranteed to get the space?

 Unfortunately not. Due to demand, many spaces are popular and not every potential renter will get the space they initially wanted. Submitting an enquiry serves as an expression of interest from you to the host about the space they have listed but it doesn’t guarantee you to the space!

I only want to rent my space out certain times of the week, can I block off space availabilities?

 When you are listing your space, you will be in total control of how long a potential renter can use your space. Set it to hourly, daily, weekly or monthly rental periods. Want to block of specific times for your space availability? Simply communicate this in your space description or use the exceptions section if you’re using our booking system.

I'd like to speak to the potential guest before we sign the license agreement, is that possible?

 You’ll have the opportunity to communicate with your potential renter when they submit an enquiry through Spacenow. From there, it is completely up to you how much or little communication you have with the potential renter.

I’d like to increase/ decrease the price of rent. How can I do this?

 Login with your user details, click on your user name once it appears after signing in top right. Use the drop down menu to locate “My Spaces” then select the workspace you wish to edit (if you have multiple listings). Select edit then run through the same steps as listing, all details will still be there just edit the price on the pricing section.

Will my account with Spacenow be secure?

 Maintaining payment security is serious. The security of cardholder data diligently follows the PCI Data Security Standards.

Are there quality checks or information about the potential guest available to me?

 There are no official quality checks for hosts to check potential renters. It’s important to use our rating system after bookings have been completed to help qualify the Spacenow community. An email will be sent to you one hour after the booking is complete, to rate and provide feedback for your guest. They will also be rating you!

Can I bring my own furniture into the space?

No you cannot unless you have written consent from the owner of the space, or it is listed in the house rules as acceptable.

I’ve caused damage to the space whilst in possession of it, what should I do?

 Under the license agreement, you are responsible for the repair or replacement of the damage. Please contact your host as soon as possible to arrange repair works, do not carry out any alteration to the space without the consent or notification of your host.

There’s damage and faults with the space out of my control (such as electrical wiring, technical faults), am I expected to pay for the repair?

 Contact your host as soon as possible. As the damage was not inflicted by you, the host will ensure proper repair works are conducted to ensure your safety. Do not attempt to arrange repair works personally, do not carry out any alteration to the space without the consent or notification of your host.

I’ve lost my keys/ access device to the space. What should I do? Who should I call?

 Contact your host as soon as possible. Do not attempt to enter the premises forcefully. As the keys/ access device was entrusted to you by the host, you will incur the cost of any replacements or repairs.

Can I put up signs and advertising for my business in the space I’ve rented?

 No you cannot unless you have written consent from the owner of the space, or it is listed in the house rules as acceptable.

If I’ve been licensed to a space for a long period of time, does this mean I am in a lease agreement with the space’s landlord?

 No. The license agreement is between yourself and the host. It does not override any pre-existing agreements between the host and the host’s landlord.

What should I do if a dispute arises between myself and a guest/host?

 Please refer to the Host Terms and the License Agreement first. If the dispute relates to any of the terms outlined in either document then they will dictate the appropriate action. For any disputes that are not outlined in the terms contact support@spacenow.com.au

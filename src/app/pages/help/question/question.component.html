How Spacenow Works

1

Search

Search amongst the hundred of spaces we have to find your ideal space in the area you want to work in.

2

Book

Book your space for the time and dates you're after, you'll receive a booking confirmation or a reply from the host once the booking is confirmed.

3

Arrive & enjoy

You're ready to go! Show up the time you booked for, enjoy your space! Once the booking is over you can leave feedback about your experience for others to see.

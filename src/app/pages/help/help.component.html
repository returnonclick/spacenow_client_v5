How will my payments be processed? Will it be secure?

Payment is extremely secure, and is processed through one of our merchants; they are Paypal, Braintree stripe & blockchain.

Can I change my booking time/date before my booking?

You can but you will lose the money you have paid. Be sure to review the Cancellation & Refunds Policy for the relevant notice periods for changes and cancellations to bookings.

How do I list my space onto the site?

To list your space with us, simply create an account by either signing into your Facebook/ Google account or create a new one with us. Proceed to, List Space. There you can enter details about the name, location, amenities available, price and availability of your space. To encourage more potential renters, we recommend you upload high quality accompanying pictures to showcase your space. Once you have filled out the form, click Submit and the Spacenow team will review your listing before it goes live on the site.

How many pictures should I upload for my listing?

We know every space is different, that’s why we recommend you upload as many pictures as you think accurately communicates the size, feel and quality of your space. We recommend between 3 & 12 high quality images. 3 is the minimum.

What support will I get from Spacenow about listing difficulties?

Phone and email support. Email us on support@spacenow.com.au or call us on 1300 720 464

What is the cost of listing my space on Spacenow?

Listing your space on Spacenow is completely FREE. Only after you have entered into a license agreement with a renter will Spacenow take a small percentage of the rent paid to you. Fees are outlined in the Host Terms.

Why should I rent from Spacenow instead of the big coworking spaces?

Spacenow lists a variety of spaces from dedicated desks to function rooms. Our hosts also range from big coworking spaces to everyday people and workers who have unused space and would like to meet new people. Finding a space with us means a greater variety in terms of location and prices.

What’s the minimum and maximum amount of time I can rent a space?

The terms for rental will be agreed between yourself and your host. We know everyone’s needs are different, that’s why there’s real flexibility for hosts and renters in terms of rental period. We have spaces available for hourly, daily, weekly, monthly or even longer rent. It really is down to your needs and the discretion of your host.

I don't have time to respond to booking enquiries, is there an option for guests to book instantly?

Yes there is. You can edit your space or when adding a new one select instant bookings to skip the enquiry or request bookings step.

Can I Extend my license?

Once your current license has expired, if it has an end date, you can renegotiate and re-enter the license agreement with the host. If there is no end date on the pre-existing license agreement it will continue until you or your host present an end of license notification to the other party.

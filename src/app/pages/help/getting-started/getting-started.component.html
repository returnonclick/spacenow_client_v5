I want a specific type of space, how can I find it on Spacenow?

The easiest way to find a Space is using our search function. Located on the homepage, you can filter spaces by their function and further narrow your search by entering the location you wish to find a space in. Still can’t find what you’re looking for? Don’t stress, we’re adding new listings daily. Check back regularly to see if your dream space has been listed with us.

How do I list my space onto the site?

To list your space with us, simply create an account by either signing into your Facebook/ Google account or create a new one with us. Proceed to, List Space. There you can enter details about the name, location, amenities available, price and availability of your space. To encourage more potential renters, we recommend you upload high quality accompanying pictures to showcase your space. Once you have filled out the form, click Submit and the Spacenow team will review your listing before it goes live on the site.

How long will it take for my listing to be approved?

All listings will be approved within 24hrs as long as they meet our standards.

What support will I get from Spacenow about listing difficulties?

We’ve included tips in the listing process to help out with listing your space, but if you’re still having difficulties then you can email support@spacenow.com.au or call us on 1300 720 464.

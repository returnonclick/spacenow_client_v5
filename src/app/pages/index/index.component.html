About Spacenow

Spacenow are the global pionners of the 'Activated Space Economy'. We active a near limitless range of under-utilised spaces by connecting hosts with guests all around the world. By removing the middleman, individuals gain the opportunity to access unique flexible rentals; and hosts can start monetising their spare space. Unlike other share-spaces platforms, Spacenow redefines 'commercial space' by expanding the constraints of the traditional definition, to make available a wide variety of spaces to almost anyone around the world. Join the sharing economy today and start sharing with Spacenow.

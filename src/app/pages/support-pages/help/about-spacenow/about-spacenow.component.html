Why should I rent from Spacenow instead of the big coworking spaces?

Spacenow lists a variety of spaces from dedicated desks to function rooms. Our hosts also range from big coworking spaces to everyday people and workers who have unused space and would like to meet new people. Finding a space with us means a greater variety in terms of location and prices.

My space is not an office space, can I still list it?

 Definitely! Here at Spacenow, we want you to be able to share any unused space you have. Have a spare kitchen, car space, function space, clinic room? List it with us! We have extensive categories available online, someone somewhere is looking for the space you have.

How can my space become a ‘Featured Listing’?

 Featured listings are up to the administrator's discretion.

Are there any other associated costs, aside from rent, I will have to pay to use Spacenow?

Nope! There are no hidden fees with Spacenow. You will only have to pay the amount agreed upon between yourself and the host.

How will my payments be processed? Will it be secure?

 Payment is extremely secure, and is processed through one of our merchants; they are Paypal, Braintree Stripe & Blockchain.

Founded in 2014, Spacenow is rapidly assisting the constantly evolving business landscape and assisting the traditional commercial real estate models.

    Having noticed a gap in the shared economy market for a service that offers commercially rentable space, Spacenow came about as the brainchild of a few Sydney entrepreneurs.

    Similar to online service uber, Spacenow is built on the platform business model of not owning any assets as a company, but rather allowing users to list their vacant spaces for rent to others. This business model facilitates a direct exchange between consumers (guests) and producers (hosts), allowing for global scalability.

    Spacenow offers over twenty-five different categories of spaces, spanning from office and coworking spaces to creative studios and storage and event spaces.

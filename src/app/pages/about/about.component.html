About Us

Spacenow.com is Australia's largest peer-to-peer marketplace for commercial space, linking hosts (people with space) with people who need it

Founded in 2014, Spacenow is rapidly assisting the constantly evolving business landscape and assisting the traditional commercial real estate models. Having noticed a gap in the shared economy market for a service that offers commercially rentable space, Spacenow came about as the brainchild of a few Sydney entrepreneurs.

Similar to online service Uber, Spacenow is built on the platform business model of not owning any assets as a company, but rather allowing users to list their vacant spaces for rent to others. This business model facilitates a direct exchange between consumers (guests) and producers (hosts), allowing for global scalability.
